2. **Audience Segmentation & Intent Engineering** — Cluster audiences by city, service and search intent; engineer pages and keywords matching commercial, navigational and AI-prompt patterns.
3. **Brand Positioning & Entity Authority Building** — NAP consistency, schema, niche links, branded mentions and entity associations on Google so the brand becomes the answer in its category.
4. **Multi-Channel Visibility Strategy** — Search + AI + Local + Social. Semantic, AI-ready content clusters per service & suburb — engineered for AIO, LLM search, Maps and brand-aligned social.
5. **Conversion & Revenue Optimization System** — Reviews, posts, GBP updates, CRO on landing pages and tracked grid scans — month-over-month compounding calls, bookings and revenue.
